QoE Observations

1. The books are materially correct. After adjusting the GL for the $1.6M non-cash TAX AJE #1, bank net (+$2.88M) and adjusted GL net (+$2.88M) differ by only $4,991 — a 0.17% variance. For QoE purposes, this is a clean tie.

2. There is a -$700K unexplained item during the Sep 2023 bank switchover. This amount is included in the bank data total but is not found in any bank statement. It occurred during a period when TTI was switching BMO accounts. For QoE, this needs explanation — it may represent a transfer between accounts that was double-counted or a data extraction error.

3. BMO-Park is off-GL. BMO-TTIPark (#696) generated +$85,248 net across the period but has no corresponding GL cash account. For QoE, this means ~$85K of cash movement was never recorded in the general ledger. This is not necessarily an error — it may be a non-operating account — but it should be disclosed.

4. 2024 is the noisy year. Month-to-month variances swing wildly in 2024 because that is when TTI opened the CNB account and began transferring significant balances between BMO and CNB. The GL records these as they are booked; the bank records them as they clear. Over the full year, bank net (-$301K) and adjusted GL net (-$210K) are directionally aligned once the $1.6M non-cash TAX AJE is removed.

5. The BMO GL cash account is contaminated by an income account. The GL "BMO Corporate Account - Other" activity summary shows a net change of -$366,872 (from $1,547,440 to $1,180,568) — but the balance sheet proves the actual cash delta is +$1,231,006. An income account is erroneously mapped to the BMO cash account. This is a material control weakness that was controlled for by using the BS actual cash line in the analysis above.

How We Match

Match Type Criteria
EXACT Check# + Amount + Date ±60 days + Account. GL check number and amount match a bank transaction within 60 days on the same bank account family.
CLOSE (Proximity) Amount + Date ±60 days + Account (no check# required). GL amount matches a bank transaction within 60 days on the same account family, but no shared check number.
Direction Deposits only match deposits, withdrawals only match withdrawals. Transfer bank records are direction-agnostic. Key fix: GL "Payment" with Split="Accounts Receivable" is treated as INFLOW (matches deposits), while Payment with Split="Accounts Payable" is treated as OUTFLOW (matches withdrawals).
NO MATCH No bank transaction found matching the GL entry within the 60-day search window.
Parameters: Date window = ±60 days. Amount tolerance = $0.00 (exact match). Account mapping enforced: BMO GL → BMO bank only; CNB GL → CNB bank only; TK GL → TK bank only.
