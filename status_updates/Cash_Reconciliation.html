QoE Observations

1. There is a -$207K variance between bank and actual cash. The balance sheet proves actual cash grew by +$4.49M (BMO +$1.23M, CNB +$3.26M), but bank statements show +$4.29M (BMO +$849K, CNB +$3.44M). BMO actual exceeds bank by $382K; CNB bank exceeds actual by $175K. Net: -$207K. The gap narrowed from -$745K after adding the missing CNB Dec 2024 deposit (+$538K).

2. BMO-Park is off-GL. BMO-TTIPark (#696) generated +$84,759 net across the period but has no corresponding GL cash account. For QoE, this means ~$85K of cash movement was never recorded in the general ledger. This is not necessarily an error — it may be a non-operating account — but it should be disclosed.

3. 2024 is the noisy year. Month-to-month variances swing wildly in 2024 (Feb +$570K, Dec -$986K) because that is when TTI opened the CNB account and began transferring significant balances between BMO and CNB. The GL records these as they are booked; the bank records them as they clear. Over the full year, bank net (-$0.17M) and GL net (+$1.40M) diverge mainly due to the $1.6M non-cash year-end tax accrual.

4. The BMO GL cash account is contaminated by an income account. The GL "BMO Corporate Account - Other" activity summary shows a net change of -$366,872 (from $1,547,440 to $1,180,568) — but the balance sheet proves the actual cash delta is +$1,231,006. An income account is erroneously mapped to the BMO cash account. This is a material control weakness but was controlled for in the variance analysis above.

How We Match

Match Type Criteria
EXACT Check# + Amount + Date ±60 days + Account. GL check number and amount match a bank transaction within 60 days on the same bank account family.
CLOSE (Proximity) Amount + Date ±60 days + Account (no check# required). GL amount matches a bank transaction within 60 days on the same account family, but no shared check number.
Direction Deposits only match deposits, withdrawals only match withdrawals. Transfer bank records are direction-agnostic. Key fix: GL "Payment" with Split="Accounts Receivable" is treated as INFLOW (matches deposits), while Payment with Split="Accounts Payable" is treated as OUTFLOW (matches withdrawals).
NO MATCH No bank transaction found matching the GL entry within the 60-day search window.
Parameters: Date window = ±60 days. Amount tolerance = $0.00 (exact match). Account mapping enforced: BMO GL → BMO bank only; CNB GL → CNB bank only; TK GL → TK bank only.
