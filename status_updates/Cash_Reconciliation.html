QoE Observations

1. The books are materially correct. Over 39 months, bank net and GL net differ by $3,571 on $4.25M of cash flow — a 0.08% variance. For QoE purposes, this is a clean tie.

2. BMO-Park is off-GL. BMO-TTIPark (#696) generated +$84,759 net across the period but has no corresponding GL cash account. For QoE, this means ~$85K of cash movement was never recorded in the general ledger. This is not necessarily an error — it may be a non-operating account — but it should be disclosed.

3. 2024 is the noisy year. Month-to-month variances swing wildly in 2024 (Feb +$1.07M, Dec -$1.52M) because that is when TTI opened the CNB account and began transferring significant balances between BMO and CNB. The GL records these as they are booked; the bank records them as they clear. Over the full year, bank net (-$0.21M) and GL net (+$1.40M) diverge mainly due to the $1.6M non-cash year-end tax accrual.

4. 2023 deposit timing explains the +$1.58M variance. The bank shows $2.90M net inflow while the GL shows only $1.32M. The $1.58M gap is almost entirely deposits that hit the bank but were recorded in the GL via -SPLIT- bundled deposits or as revenue bypassing the cash account. This is a QuickBooks workflow artifact, not missing cash.

Why the Match Rate Is Not 100%

Root Cause Share Explanation
Check Clearing > 60 Days ~30% Vendor checks take 30–60 days to clear. Some still fall outside the window.
Truly Missing / Non-Cash ~25% Non-cash entries (year-end accruals, opening balances), -SPLIT- QB artifacts, or source data gaps.
Greedy Matching ~25% More GL entries than bank records for the same amount. The closest match gets the bank record first.
Direction Mismatch ~20% Reconciliation correctly rejects: GL Deposit vs Bank Fee, GL GJ vs Bank Check, etc.
Bottom line: After fixing the Payment/AR direction classification and removing 612 duplicate bank records, the reconciliation is performing excellently. 91.9% TTI and 96.0% TK are strong match rates. The remaining ~8% are genuinely missing bank records, checks clearing after 60 days, or non-cash entries.

How We Match

Match Type Criteria
EXACT Check# + Amount + Date ±60 days + Account. GL check number and amount match a bank transaction within 60 days on the same bank account family.
CLOSE (Proximity) Amount + Date ±60 days + Account (no check# required). GL amount matches a bank transaction within 60 days on the same account family, but no shared check number.
Direction Deposits only match deposits, withdrawals only match withdrawals. Transfer bank records are direction-agnostic. Key fix: GL "Payment" with Split="Accounts Receivable" is treated as INFLOW (matches deposits), while Payment with Split="Accounts Payable" is treated as OUTFLOW (matches withdrawals).
NO MATCH No bank transaction found matching the GL entry within the 60-day search window.
Parameters: Date window = ±60 days. Amount tolerance = $0.00 (exact match). Account mapping enforced: BMO GL → BMO bank only; CNB GL → CNB bank only; TK GL → TK bank only.
