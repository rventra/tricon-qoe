The Big Picture

TK recorded $8.7M in GL entries across four years. Of that, $4.9M (56%) matched to bank.

What's Driving the Gaps?

2023 — $101K unmatched (11 items)

TK's best year. Only 11 items didn't match, and they're mostly timing differences — entries from late December 2022 or early January 2023 that fell just outside our 45-day matching window. One small equity adjustment ($6K) is non-cash.

2024 — $2.5M unmatched (210 items)

2024 TK-Investments bank statements are now received. The 210 previously unmatched items can now be re-processed through the matching engine. All items are real cash transactions awaiting bank-side verification.

The original 3 incidental matches ($5.4K) will be replaced with full engine results once re-processing is complete.

2025 — $363K unmatched (18 items)

Back to solid ground. The 18 gaps are a mix of timing differences and a few intercompany quirks where TK and TTI may record the same transaction slightly differently. Nothing concerning.

2026 — $865K unmatched (78 items)

The cash account itself (T-K Investments LLC) is matching at 72.1% — reasonable for a partial year with current bank data.

The rest of the unmatched amount comes from non-cash GL accounts that simply don't have a bank equivalent:

— Gross Trucking Income ($338K): Revenue bookings — no bank transaction expected.

— Payroll Expenses ($316K): Expense entries — the bank shows the net clearing amount, not the individual payroll lines.

— 401k, utilities, and other adjustments ($20K): Various non-cash entries.

Unlike TTI, the TK GL feed includes all account types — not just cash. So a 0% match on revenue or payroll is actually correct.

How We Match

Same approach as TTI:

— Exact: Same check number + same amount + within about 45 days.

— Close: Same amount + close date, even if the check number doesn't line up.

We search across all four years at once, so cross-year timing isn't a dead end.
